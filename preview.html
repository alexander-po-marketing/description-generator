Clinical overview

Well-established GLP-1 agonist
⌄

Improves glycemic control, promotes weight loss, and lowers cardiovascular risk in adults with type 2 diabetes.

  • Demonstrated HbA1c reductions of ~1-1.5%
  • Lower incidence of hypoglycemia versus insulin
  • Weekly dosing improves adherence
